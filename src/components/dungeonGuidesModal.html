Help

Dungeon guides will automatically guide you through the dungeon.

They will automatically open any chest they come accross, go down ladders and start boss battles.

You must pay an upfront cost for however many clears you want to run of the dungeon (non refundable), they will however give you a discount the more runs you purchase in one go.

Make sure you keep enough Dungeon Tokens for the dungeon costs as if you run out, your runs will be over.

Some move faster than others, some have a sense where specific dungeon tiles are located and some will avoid specific tiles where possible
